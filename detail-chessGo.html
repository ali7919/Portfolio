Chess Go

  • Category: Unity
  • Start Date: September 2021 (Mehr 1400)
  • Project URL: CafeBazaar

Chess Go is a chess-like strategy game combined with the endless runner genre and some other twists. It is my first published game which is made with Unity. I made the game all by myself in the span of 4 months without using any premade assets (except for sounds) and made everything from scratch, I used Blender for 3d modeling, UV editing, and texturing. 2d sprites were made using Aseprite Or Figma. I used Audacity for editing sound assets and made its teaser entirely in Blender, Posters and UI prototypes were made using Figma and I managed it all in Trello. One of the most challenging parts of it was the AI which I programmed it based on a Min-MAx approach and I also implemented Unity’s new Analytics SDK. Chess go was published on CafeBazaar the biggest app market in Iran and was chosen by CafeBazaar to be listed on the home page.
