  • Category: Unity
  • Start Date: April 2022 (Farvardin 1401)

2 demo projects around unity ar :

- first one is a simple boss fight . It starts with a sequence , the player opens a chest full of gems then the boss appears with one attack type (shooting missiles) and the player can also shoot back at it. the player , boss and its missiles have certain amount of health which decreases by being shot at.

- second one is an ar credit card , the card spawns on the scanned image on the actual credit card and sticks to it. the visuals are achieved using shaders (stencil buffer).
